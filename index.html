About Me

Currently working as an Associate Prompt Engineer at NVIDIA, I specialize in optimizing large language models and exploring the cutting edge of AI video generation.

My passion lies in solving real-world challenges with technology. As the founder of OceanMind, an EEG-based mood prediction app, and lead developer of a HIPAA-compliant healthcare NLP chatbot, I've demonstrated my ability to translate innovative ideas into impactful solutions. I believe in continuous innovation and bringing bold ideas to life. My expertise goes beyond theory—I'm a builder who architects complete solutions from concept to reality, delivering scalable tech innovations that create real value.
